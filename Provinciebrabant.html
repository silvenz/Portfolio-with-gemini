Noord-Brabant is a province in the south of the Netherlands. The capital is 's-Hertogenbosch. North Brabant has approximately 2.5 million inhabitants and is therefore in third place in the Netherlands. In terms of area, the province is the largest after Gelderland.

Brabant has the ambition to be energy neutral by 2050. To achieve this, the province, together with 4 municipalities, has taken the initiative to realize wind energy along the A16. To help decide the exact position of this windpark, the provence is obligated to release an environmental impact assessment. We were asked to help with the digitalisation of this report.

We designed a digital environmental impact assessment for the provence. Completely connected to GIS, the application has the ability to present the different solutions and the effects to the stakeholders.

Gathering requirements, User research, Stakeholder management, Wireframes, Responsive design, Prototyping, Accessibility, UX/UI Design
