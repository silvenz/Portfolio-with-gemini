Tetra Pak is a Swedish-Swiss multinational food packaging and processing company with head offices in Lund, Sweden, and Pully, Switzerland. Tetra Pak is the largest food packaging company in the world by sales, operating in more than 160 countries and with over 24,800 employees (2017).

Tetra Pak is one of the world’s three main producers of evaporators. They build them up to 20 metres high with fans driven by motors at 600 – 700 Kw. But what makes Tetrapak stand out from the crowd is the level of expertise among their evaporation specialists. Hygienic equipment design and gentle process treatment are necessary to get a high quality end product while avoiding contamination. The evaporation process and associated pre-heating has a significant impact on the desired functionality and microbiology of the powder.

Tetra Pak had the ambition to grow its whey production. We were asked to help them with their evaporation & drying strategy in order to get their engineers on the same page.
