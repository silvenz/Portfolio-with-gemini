Windstats

UX/UI design

Role
UX/UI designer
Company
Windstats
Year
2020-2021

Windstats is a SAAS platform displaying all the wind energy statistics of the Netherlands. The platform gives companies such as TNO, Provincie Zuid Holland and Centraal Bureau Voor de Statistiek insight in the added wind energy capacity in the Netherlands.

We have helped Windstats to design a product which makes it easy to request and compare data from all wind farms and wind turbines in the Netherlands. A WindStats account offers you up-to-date information about wind turbines in the Netherlands.

You can make and save your own selections, for example per park, municipality, province, manufacturer or type of turbine. Details of the selections are available in different types of graphs and charts. The selected data is easy to export in CSV format.

Activities and deliverables

Gathering requirements, User research, Stakeholder management, Wireframes, Responsive design, Prototyping, Accessibility, UX/UI Design
