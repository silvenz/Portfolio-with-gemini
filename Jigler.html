Jigler

Creating the best experience for job seekers and employers

Role
UX/UI Design
Company
P-Services
Year
2019

P-services is a specialist in the field of personnel management. They are a reliable partner in temporary employment, payrolling, salary administration, HR advice and absent management. Good employment practices are paramount in everything they do.

P-Services is a company that is constantly evolving. Their next step was to, in addition to payrolling, to work as an employment agency. This not only required a different set-up of the organization, but the P-Services consultants would also benefit from a new platform where supply and demand come together.

To help P-Services, we did a brand and UX/UI track in parallel. From initial positioning and making it fit with the values of P-Services to visual design. Jigler became more than a job posting platform. They make the selection, the match and provide interesting employment conditions for the employees during their career.

Activities and deliverables

Gathering requirements, User research, journey mapping, Wireframes, Responsive design, Prototyping, UX/UI Design, Visual design, Illustration
