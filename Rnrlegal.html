RNR Legal & Consulting is a legal and business affairs services company, based in Utrecht. They specialize in legal advice for companies, buy outs, IP & licenses, clearances, agreements, establishing a foreign branch, brand and intellectual property advice.

After being part of MediaMonks for years, It was time to spread the wings and setup a new brand. RNR Legal knows better than anyone what challenges you face as a young and growing company. Does your brand need protection? Are agreements needed? Do you need legal advice for your production? Do you want to expand abroad? With their network of lawyers and specialists all over the world, they can assist you in many areas.

WWe helped RNR Legal with a review of the brand, did research on the target audience and points of differentiation.
