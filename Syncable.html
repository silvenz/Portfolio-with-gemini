Syncable

Brand designer, Co-owner, UX/UI Designer

Role
Brand designer, Co-owner, UX/UI Designer
Company
Syncable
Year
2017

Syncable is a platform filled with the best high-quality music from international artists and top composers, created by artists, for video editors.

A lot of video producers are facing the problem of the time-consuming process to find and acquire the rights connected to music tracks. On the other hand there is a lot of unfair profit distribution to rightsholders, in the sense of its fairness towards quality and labor.

Within a multidisciplinair team we created a brand an a platform which aims to offer the best music, bringing innovation and uniting two creative worlds. Syncable is meant for video editors searching high-quality music on an easy and accessible platform. With Syncable you can purchase a license and enjoy unlimited access to an extensive music library. Enabling you to filter, browse and find that perfect sync for the online video.

Branding, Gathering requirements, User research, Working in Scrum Agile teams, Wireframes, Responsive design, UX/UI Design
